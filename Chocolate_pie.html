Chocolate Pie

The best chocolate pie you have never had!

This chocolate pie was created in a bakery in Asheville, North Carolina. It has been a staple dish for the bakery for over 65 years.

You can use this recipe when you want to wow your guests with an authentic tasting chocolate pie.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole pie-crust, baked and cooled Use an Oreo or graham cracker crust for added flavor
  • 1 1/2 c. sugar
  • 1/4 c. cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 3 c. whole milk
  • 4 whole egg yolks
  • 6 1/2 oz. weight bittersweet chocolate chop finely
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp. butter KerryGold
  • whipped cream For Serving

Steps to Perfection

  1. Combine sugar, cornstarch, and salt in a medium saucepan and whisk together.
  2. Pour in milk and egg yolks, and whisk together.
  3. Stir over medium heat for 6-8 minutes.the second it starts to bubble,take it off the heat.
  4. Add the chocolate, vanilla, and butter, and stir until mixed well
  5. Pour the pudding into the pie crust and place in the fridge to chill for 4 hours.Be sure to save any extra pudding in small dishes!
  6. The only step left is to cut your pie into slices for you and your guests to enjoy!
